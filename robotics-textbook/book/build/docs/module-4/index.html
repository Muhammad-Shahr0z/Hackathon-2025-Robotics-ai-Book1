Module 4: Advanced Topics and Real-World Deployment

Focus: NVIDIA Isaac, VLA models, and hardware deployment.

Overview​

This module introduces cutting-edge tools and techniques for training robots in simulation and deploying them to real hardware.

Chapters​

Chapter 10: NVIDIA Isaac Sim and Omniverse​

Leverage photorealistic simulation for training and testing.

Chapter 11: Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models​

Integrate large language models with robot control.

Chapter 12: Deploying to Real Hardware​

Transfer your simulated systems to physical robots.

Module Duration​

Estimated Time: 2-3 weeks (Weeks 11-12 of the course)
Difficulty: Advanced

Getting Started​

Begin with Chapter 10: NVIDIA Isaac Sim.
